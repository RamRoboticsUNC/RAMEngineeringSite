Inside RamEngineering

RamEngineering is a club, but we emulate an ecosystem where students design, test, fail, rebuild, and launch real technology. From deep rocketry and advanced mechatronics to custom PCBs, our members operate like a startup lab within UNC.

Built by Builders

Students collaborate in engineering spaces across campus, learning hands-on skills that go far beyond the classroom. You will prototype, solder, code, fabricate, and iterate alongside some of the most driven students at Carolina.

Our Vision

RamEngineering unifies builders here at Carolina under a single identity. We design for autonomy, intelligence, precision, and scale.
