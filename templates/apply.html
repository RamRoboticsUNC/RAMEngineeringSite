FAQs

What positions are you looking for?

We are looking for team members for Project teams, Operations team, and the Finance team!

Can I apply to multiple divisions? Do I need to apply separately?

Yes! You can apply to any division(s) you would like to, and they all have their own separate applications so you'll apply to them individually, even for operations & finance.

How do I apply to be an exec?

Execs are chosen through internal voting available to all club members in the spring. (Execs are appointed for the academic year)

How do Operations & Finance teams work?

Since the club is new, all branches are managed by Ram Engineering's Operation and Finance team and don't have their own (since RE is technically the official club). All of the branch applications have Operations/Finance roles available but they are all combined in the end!
